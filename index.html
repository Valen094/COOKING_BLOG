How to make KEBAB

This kebab recipe originates from southern Turkey, where it is known as Adana Kebab. Traditionally, it is prepared with hand-minced lamb, for which a fatty meat is usually chosen that adheres properly to the swords or skewers and for this it is common to use lamb tail. Enjoy this delicious Middle Eastern dish, you will love it!

ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon coriander seed
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seed
  • 1 teaspoon chili flake
  • 2 garlic cloves, shelled
  • 400 grams of ground lamb, with lamb tail fat
  • 1 cup red bell pepper, finely chopped, drain as much as possible to remove excess water
  • 1 teaspoon paprika to taste of salt to taste pepper
  • 1 cup of cherry tomato, roasted over charcoal, to accompany
  • 1/2 cups red onion, in strips, to accompany

Preparation

  1. Crush the coriander seed, cumin and chile flakes in a molcajete or mortar until you get a powder. Add the garlic and crush to form a reddish paste. Booking.
  2. Combine ground lamb with drained red bell pepper, paprika, garlic paste, and enough salt and pepper in a bowl; integrates perfectly. Remember that due to the amount of fat that the meat has, it will be very manageable. In many parts of Turkey they let this preparation rest for a whole night, you can do it if you wish.
  3. Take 3 long wooden skewers and stick a little of the meat mixture with your hands to each one, you will notice how easy it will be to manipulate. Press to adhere the meat and give it an elongated shape. Repeat until you finish the meat.
  4. Cook the kebabs on a hot charcoal grill for 10 minutes, turning regularly. Also, remember that the characteristic of this dish is that on the outside it usually looks like dried meat, but on the inside it must be very juicy meat. Once ready, remove the kebabs from the charcoal.
  5. Serve the kebabs with roasted tomato, red onion and roasted green chiles. Remember to include pita bread and yogurt sauce to accompany.

MEAT SKEWERS

These meat and potato skewers have the perfect balance between the creaminess of the potatoes, the succulent flavor of the meat and the freshness of the salad, they will be a success at lunchtime!

ingredients

  • 1 cup of Kikkoman® soy sauce, for the marinade
  • 1 tablespoon chili flakes, for the marinade
  • 1 tablespoon yellow lemon zest, for the marinade
  • 500 grams of beef fillet
  • 2 pumpkins, sliced thin and upright
  • Enough vegetable oil
  • Enough olive oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, sliced
  • 2 cups potato, sliced ​​½ cm, with skin

Preparation

  1. Mix Kikkoman® Soy Sauce, chili flakes and lemon zest in a bowl.
  2. Add the beef fillet to the marinade and let sit for an hour.
  3. Assemble the skewers by interspersing the meat and the pumpkin slices in an "S" shape.
  4. Heat a skillet or grill over medium heat, brush the skewers with a little oil and cook for approximately 10 minutes, making sure to sear each side.
  5. For the potatoes: Heat abundant olive oil in a skillet over low heat, fry the garlic for 2 minutes, remove and add the potatoes, cook them for 10 minutes or until they are fully cooked and soft, without breaking. Remove from the oil and pass them through absorbent paper.
  6. To assemble the dish: Place the meat skewers on a bed of potatoes and arugula salad to decorate accompanied with Kikkoman® Soy Sauce.
